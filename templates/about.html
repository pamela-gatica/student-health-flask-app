About the Dataset

This project uses the Student Health Data dataset from Kaggle. It includes wellness and workload-related indicators that can be used to explore student health patterns and potential risk levels.

Dataset source: Kaggle - Student Health Data

The original dataset contains 1,000 rows and 14 columns. This web application displays a sample of 100 records for easier viewing.

Variables Included

  • Student_ID: Unique student identifier
  • Age, Gender: Demographic information
  • Heart_Rate: Physiological heart rate measurement
  • Blood_Pressure_Systolic / Blood_Pressure_Diastolic: Blood pressure values
  • Stress_Level_Biosensor: Stress level measured through sensors
  • Stress_Level_Self_Report: Self-reported stress score
  • Physical_Activity: Low, Moderate, or High
  • Sleep_Quality: Poor, Moderate, or Good
  • Mood: Happy, Neutral, or Stressed
  • Study_Hours / Project_Hours: Workload-related time variables
  • Health_Risk_Level: Low, Moderate, or High

About the Database

Dataset source, context, and field-level documentation.

Source

Kaggle — Student Health Data

kaggle.com/datasets/ziya07/student-health-data

Description
“This file, student_health_data.csv, contains data for assessing health risks among college students involved in academic and entrepreneurial activities. The data is structured to support machine learning models that predict health conditions related to stress, physical strain, and mental well-being. Each row represents an individual student’s health, workload, and lifestyle characteristics.”
Observations

Sample displayed on this app

The original dataset contains 1,000 rows and 14 columns. This web application displays a sample of 100 rows with all original columns.

Data Structure

Field Descriptions

Student_ID — Unique identifier for each student.
Age / Gender — Demographic information relevant to physiological data interpretation.
Heart_Rate — Heart rate measurement.
Blood_Pressure_Systolic / Blood_Pressure_Diastolic — Blood pressure indicators.
Stress_Level_Biosensor / Stress_Level_Self_Report — Measured and self-reported stress levels.
Physical_Activity — Low, Moderate, or High.
Sleep_Quality — Poor, Moderate, or Good.
Mood — Happy, Neutral, or Stressed.
Study_Hours / Project_Hours — Weekly academic and project workload.
Health_Risk_Level — Low, Moderate, or High health risk category.
